HOW IT WORKS

Four Simple Steps to Draft-Quality Pours

Set it up, chill it down, pressurize, and pour like you own the lot.

FILL

Load your 1-gallon pressure tanks with your beer or beverage of choice.

CHILL

Place tanks in cooler with ice, or pre-chill in fridge the night before.

PRESSURIZE

Attach the CO2 regulator — one click to pressurize and you are ready.

POUR

Pull the tap handle and enjoy a perfect cold draft-quality pour every time.

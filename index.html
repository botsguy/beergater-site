Our Story

Built for the Long Haul

It all started with our idea to create a smaller, more portable tailgating beer dispenser because we were tired of dealing with renting a keg every time we wanted to have a decent tailgate. We couldn't find anything in the marketplace, so we needed to innovate. After lots of 'garage' engineering and field testing, we finally had a working product. After much more innovation and better engineering by design professionals, the BeerGater pressure tanks perform like champs!

Origin Story

The Birth of Our Beloved BeerGater

Believe it or not, the idea for BeerGater originally stemmed from the inventor dressing up for a Halloween party as a self-sufficient beer dispensing, walking bartender where guests could pull a cold beer from a tap he was wearing. When he got tired of walking around, he put it on a table where guests could still pull a beer from it - and BeerGater was born. Glorious. BeerGater is meant to be with you for the long haul.

How It Works

1

FILL

Load your 1-gallon pressure tanks with your beer or beverage of choice.

2

CHILL

Place tanks inside your cooler with ice, or pre-chill in the fridge overnight.

3

PRESSURIZE

Attach the CO2 regulator — a simple click is all it takes to pressurize.

4

POUR

Pull the tap and enjoy a perfect, cold, draft-quality pour every time.
